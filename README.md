
# Predicting Monthly Pneumonia Admissions in Under-5 Children in Bangladesh (Winter 2026)

## Overview
This project focuses on forecasting pneumonia admissions for children under 5 years old in Bangladesh during the upcoming winter (Dec 2026 – Feb 2027). Pneumonia is a leading cause of hospitalization in young children, especially during cold months. Accurate predictions help hospitals plan resources, staffing, and preventive healthcare measures.

## Project Goals
1. Analyze historical trends in pneumonia admissions during winter months.
2. Examine the impact of environmental and hospital-related factors.
3. Build predictive models to forecast monthly pneumonia admissions for winter 2026.
4. Provide actionable insights for hospitals and public health authorities.

## Dataset
- Source: Synthetic dataset generated from publicly available health reports for academic purposes.
- Rows: 3120 (monthly records over 10 years)
- Columns: 14, including Month, District, Gender, Age_Months, Pneumonia_Admissions_U5, environmental factors, hospital factors, Winter_Season_Flag.
- Notes: Some missing and faulty values were intentionally introduced for preprocessing exercises.
